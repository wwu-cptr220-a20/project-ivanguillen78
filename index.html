Topic Overview

Discovering new music can sometimes be a hassle. People will often find themselves growing tired of hearing the same few songs and will try to find new music. However, this isn't always as easy it seems. For example, people are clearly looking for new music within a certain genre. Likewise, they could be looking for music that is similar, but slightly different than what they're used to. Of course, you could click around and hopefully find a few songs that fit what you're searching for. However, many people either do not have the time to do so or don't want to.

There are apps, such as Spotify, that create personalized playlists based on what you listen to. However, I have found that the playlists are typically made up of songs that you already listen to, with a few others sprinkled in. Personally, I don't like these playlists. From my point of view, they often take a few songs within the same genre and make a playlist based on these songs. However, the songs they add to playlist aren't songs I would normally listen to or enjoy. Other apps, such as Apple Music will simply suggest playlists based on genres you listen to.
